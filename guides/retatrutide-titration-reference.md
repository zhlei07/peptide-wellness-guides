# Retatrutide Titration Reference

A practical, citation-friendly reference page for retatrutide users and researchers. Last updated 2026-05.

## What Retatrutide Is

Retatrutide (LY3437943) is a triple receptor agonist acting on **GLP-1, GIP and glucagon** receptors. The glucagon component is the meaningful structural difference from tirzepatide (GLP-1 + GIP) and semaglutide (GLP-1 only). Glucagon receptor activation increases hepatic energy expenditure and lipolysis.

Eli Lilly phase II data (NEJM 2023) showed mean weight reduction at 48 weeks at the 12 mg dose meaningfully exceeding tirzepatide at comparable timepoints.

## Recommended Titration Window

| Phase | Dose | Duration | Goal |
|------|------|----------|------|
| Initiation | 2 mg/week | Weeks 1–4 | Build GI tolerance |
| Step 1 | 4 mg/week | Weeks 5–8 | Consolidate response |
| Step 2 | 6 mg/week | Weeks 9–12 | Most steady-state plateau |
| Step 3 | 8 mg/week | Weeks 13–16 | Optional, response-driven |
| Step 4 | 10 mg/week | Weeks 17–20 | Optional |
| Step 5 | 12 mg/week | Weeks 21+ | Trial maximum |

**Hold rule:** if a step is rough, repeat that same step. Do not drop. The body acclimates against exposure, not against backwards motion.

## Reconstitution

Retatrutide is typically supplied as lyophilized powder in 10 mg, 30 mg, or 60 mg vials. Standard reconstitution uses **bacteriostatic water** for a 28-day refrigerated stability window.

| Vial size | BAC water | Concentration | 2 mg dose volume |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|------------------|
| 10 mg | 1.0 mL | 10 mg/mL | 0.20 mL (20 units) |
| 30 mg | 3.0 mL | 10 mg/mL | 0.20 mL (20 units) |
| 60 mg | 3.0 mL | 20 mg/mL | 0.10 mL (10 units) |

**Mixing rules:**
1. Run BAC water down the side of the vial.
2. Swirl gently for 30 seconds.
3. Never shake. Shearing degrades the peptide.

## Side Effect Management

The phase II discontinuation rate due to AEs was higher than for tirzepatide. Most of that is avoidable.

- **Reduce meal size before increasing the dose**, not after. People who maintain pre-titration meal volume after a dose step are the people who experience the worst nausea.
- **Hydrate above baseline.** Triple co-agonism slows gastric emptying noticeably.
- **Hold, don't drop.** Repeating the rough step trains tolerance. Going backwards just resets the calendar.

## Comparison vs Tirzepatide

| Factor | Retatrutide | Tirzepatide |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Receptor targets | GLP-1 + GIP + glucagon | GLP-1 + GIP |
| Trial mean weight loss (peak dose) | ~24% (48wk) | ~22% (72wk) |
| FDA approval status | Phase III | Approved |
| Real-world experience base | Limited | Broad |
| Titration steepness | Steeper | Gentler |
| GI tolerance demand | Higher | Moderate |

## Storage

- Refrigerate reconstituted vials at 2–8 °C.
- 28-day usable window with bacteriostatic water; 24 hours with sterile water.
- Avoid temperature cycling. Freezing destroys the peptide; ambient temperature for >24 h compromises potency.
