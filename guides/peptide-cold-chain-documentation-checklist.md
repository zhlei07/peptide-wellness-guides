# Peptide Cold Chain Documentation Checklist

A practical reference for wholesale buyers receiving research-grade peptide shipments.

## On receipt — within 30 minutes

- Verify outer packaging is intact and not water-damaged
- Confirm cold pack is still frozen or substantially frozen
- Check temperature indicator strip (if included) shows green / in-range
- Verify vial labels match the certificate of analysis exactly
- Photograph the lyophilized cake under consistent lighting (retain 2 years)

## Within 2 hours

- Move vials to 2-8°C refrigeration
- Log lot number, receipt date, and any visual anomalies into your lot record
- Cross-reference COA against supplier invoice and any previous lots

## Red flags requiring hold

- Cold pack fully thawed AND temperature indicator tripped
- Vial labels printed differently than COA documents (potential relabeling)
- Visible particulates, yellow discoloration, or cake collapse
- Lot number not findable on either invoice or COA
